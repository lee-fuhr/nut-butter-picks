Nut butters are a pantry staple for good reason: they’re packed with healthy fats, protein, and fiber, and they work just as well on toast as they do in smoothies, sauces, or straight off the spoon. But with dozens of brands and endless claims – natural, no stir, organic, single ingredient – it’s easy to get stuck in the supermarket aisle. Choosing the right nut butter comes down to three things: ingredients, texture, and price. Do you want pure nuts and nothing else, or are you okay with a little salt, sugar, or palm oil for stability? Do you prefer a spoonable, no-stir consistency, or are you fine with stirring in the natural oil? And how much are you willing to spend?
