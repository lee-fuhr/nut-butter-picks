Nut butters are a pantry staple for quick breakfasts, post‑workout snacks, and sauces, but the price difference between a basic peanut butter and a “specialty” almond butter can be shocking. Budget nut butters prove that you don’t need to spend a premium to get a clean ingredient list – often just nuts and maybe salt. The trick is knowing which brands skip added sugars, palm oil, and stabilizers while still delivering a pleasant texture and taste. When shopping on a budget, look for jars that list exactly one ingredient (peanuts or almonds) or two (nuts plus salt). Avoid anything labeled “natural” that still includes hydrogenated oils or sugar. Price per ounce matters, but so does consistency: some pure nut butters separate and require stirring, which can be a minor hassle. The three picks below cover the full range from cheapest to most splurge‑worthy, each vetted by owner feedback and published reviews for value, taste, and texture.
